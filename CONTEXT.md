# Project Context: SwayHouse (swayhouse.in)

This document contains the core identity, brand guidelines, business decisions, and development rules for **SwayHouse**. Any AI coding assistant starting a new session in this workspace **must** read and adhere to this file.

---

## 1. Project Overview & Philosophy
SwayHouse is a premium, minimalist **creator management agency** (not a general marketing or social media agency). 
* **Core Philosophy**: A high-touch, honest, and deep-partnership model for managing and growing talent. We prioritize custom-tailored strategies over mass signing.
* **Stage**: Pre-launch, pre-revenue.
* **Founders & Team**: Ayush (Owner / Lead Negotiator).
* **Key Creators**: Currently starting with one creator, **Aditi** (a close friend of the owner).

---

## 2. Brand Identity & Design System (Locked)
The visual identity is modeled after the premium, clean aesthetics of brands like **Apple** and **Adobe**.

* **Color Palette**:
  * **Primary Background**: Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) – strict rule, keep it clean and spacious.
  * **Primary Text**: Solid Black / Off-Black (`#111111` or `#000000`).
  * **Accent Color**: Sophisticated Vermillion / Burnt Orange (`#d95a3b`) – used sparingly for focus, icons, or subtle highlights.
  * **UI Dividers / Subtleties**: Warm-gray panels/lines (`#f9f9f9` or `#eaeaea`).
* **Typography**: Clean, geometric sans-serif (such as *Inter*, *SF Pro*, or *Outfit*). High-end layout rules: generous line-heights, ample margins, and structural grid alignment.
* **Logo**: A minimalist house outline with a thin vermillion orange horizontal wave running through it. "SwayHouse" written in bold black, with no additional subtext.

## 3. Strict Website Rules (CRITICAL)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **RULE 1: NO MONEY / FINANCIAL DETAILS**
> Under no circumstances should the website mention money, commissions, pricing tiers, percentages, or payment structures. Ayush handles all commercial negotiations privately in person.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **RULE 2: NO SPOTLIGHT FOR ADITI YET**
> Do not build a creator spotlight or featured card for Aditi on the public roster page yet. Her niche is not yet defined, and she is not ready for a public showcase. 
> * **Roster Section Rule**: Keep it minimal and generic. Use copy like *"We work with a curated roster of emerging creators"* with a Call-To-Action (CTA) to apply.

* **Purpose of the Website**: Serve as a high-credibility signal for brands and creators looking up SwayHouse on search engines.
* **Core Sections Planned**:
  1. **Hero**: Strong, premium minimalist headline and primary CTA.
  2. **Our Philosophy**: Explaining our deep-partnership, value-first approach.
  3. **Services**: Outlining our 4 service pillars (Growth, Partnerships, Operations, Advisory).
  4. **Minimal Roster**: A subtle, clean statement about our creators (no specific spotlights).
  5. **Application Form**: A premium questionnaire for creators seeking management.
  6. **Footer**: Navigation, legal, and credentials.
